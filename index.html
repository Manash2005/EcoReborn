Waste to Wonder!

EcoReborn is a platform that promotes the idea of recycling and reusing waste materials to create beautiful and useful products.

We believe that waste is not waste until it is wasted. Our products are made from waste materials such as plastic, paper, glass, and metal. We aim to create awareness about the importance of recycling and reusing waste materials to protect the environment and reduce waste generation. Join us in our mission to turn waste into wonder!

Statistics

The amount of food wasted in Indian households every year could feed almost 377 million people.
India generates around 62 million tons of waste each year.
In 2022 there were 1.05 billion tonnes of food waste generated.
In India, about 43 million tons of waste are collected out of the total 62 million tons that are generated annually.
